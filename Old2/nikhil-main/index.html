About Me

Words That Make an Impact

I'm a journalist and content editor with deep roots in Kannada digital media, having contributed to some of India's most widely read regional news platforms — Hindustan Times Kannada, Times Internet's Vijay Karnataka, and Asianet News Network.

My editorial focus spans science journalism — especially astronomy, space exploration, and ISRO/NASA missions — alongside high-stakes coverage of state elections, live telecasts, and defence events like Aero India 2021.

I bring together traditional editorial rigour with modern tools: SEO content strategy, AI-assisted editorial workflows, video content, and digital audience analytics. My work has contributed to record online traffic milestones for teams I've led.
