About Me

A Journalist With a Scientist's Curiosity

I'm a Bengaluru-based content editor and journalist with over 8 years of experience across Kannada digital media. My career has taken me from live TV at Suvarna News to leading editorial teams at Hindustan Times Kannada.

I have a deep love for science journalism — translating complex topics like space missions and aerospace into compelling stories for everyday readers. I've covered state elections, attended defence ministry events, and conducted special interviews with scientists and public figures.

Whether working under deadline pressure or mentoring new writers, I believe good journalism is about clarity, accuracy, and genuine curiosity about the world.
